RTI Web Simulator

Reflectance Transformation Imaging (RTI) reveals surface details by varying light direction over a subject. Upload any image to see simulated raking-light effects.

Controls

  • Tweakpane panel — adjust light direction, intensity, color, and material properties
  • Mouse drag — orbit the camera
  • Scroll — zoom in / out

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • R — reset light to default position
  • S — save a PNG snapshot
  • E — cycle through image effects
  • H / ? — toggle this help
